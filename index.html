What's different

Built for travelers who think,
not for travelers who click.

Conflict zone filtering
Every route is checked against a live database of war zones and restricted airspace. Affected flights are filtered out or flagged with an advisory — not buried in fine print.
Natural language search
Describe the trip as you'd say it out loud. Gemini extracts airports, dates, cabin class, stop preferences, and price ceiling — no dropdowns required.
Multi-origin, multi-destination
"From Berlin" resolves to BER, TXL, and SXF. "To Tokyo" resolves to NRT and HND. All combinations are searched in one request — you see the best option, not the most obvious.
Round-trip as a pair
Round trips return as matched outbound + return pairs with a combined total price. No mental arithmetic. No comparing two separate searches. Both legs shown together, one booking decision.
